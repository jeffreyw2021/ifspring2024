Hawaiian Loco Moco

Animal Protein
Contains Gluten

Loco Moco, a beloved Hawaiian comfort dish, features layers of warm white rice, a tender hamburger patty, a perfectly fried egg, and is generously smothered in a rich, savory gravy. This culinary delight is a versatile meal, equally perfect for breakfast, lunch, or dinner, offering a taste of island comfort in every bite. Its simplicity belies its profound flavor, making it a favorite for locals and a must-try for visitors seeking an authentic Hawaiian dining experience.
